# 角色图片提示词模板

## 目标

角色提示词用于生成稳定的角色定妆图或参考图。重点是外貌连续性、表演气质和清晰主体，不是复杂背景。

## 模板结构

```text
A [age] [gender] character named [name], [body type], [face shape and facial features],
[hair style and color], [signature details],
wearing [clothing style, color, fabric, accessories],
[pose and expression], conveying [temperament/personality],
[role context if visually useful],
cinematic portrait, detailed character concept art, consistent art style,
soft clean background, high quality, sharp focus, no text, no watermark
```

## 生成要点

- 年龄、性别、体型、脸型、发型、服装必须具体。
- 性格不要只写标签，要转成姿态和表情。
- 角色图通常只出现一个角色，避免多人干扰。
- 背景保持简洁，突出角色。
- 不要写字幕、姓名牌、Logo、签名、水印。

## 示例

```text
A young woman in her early 20s named Li Mei, slender build, delicate oval face, bright almond eyes, soft but guarded expression,
long black hair with subtle waves falling over her shoulders,
wearing a vintage deep blue qipao with fine floral embroidery and pearl earrings,
standing upright with one hand lightly touching her sleeve, calm and resilient temperament,
cinematic portrait, detailed character concept art, consistent art style,
soft warm gradient background, high quality, sharp focus, no text, no watermark
```
